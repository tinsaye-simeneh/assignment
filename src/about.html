How we started

Our company started out with only 3 employees. In few years the number doubled. No with over 50 branches, we have around 300 permanent and nearly a thousand temporary employees. In the early days of our company we focused on fruits only. Especially Avocados, Mangoes, Bananas and the like.

Currently we have branches 50 branches all over the country. From Adama to Bahirdar, from Semera to Woldia any corner of the country you can think we are there.

Where we are now

With more than 500 employees and 40million$ captial we are one of the biggest agricultural product provider in africa. We surely won't stop here. We hope to improve our exports and grow even more. Creating more job opportunites and serve the community we came from is always at the forefront of all our plans.
